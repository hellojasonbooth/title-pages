Our planet is changing. The future of life on Earth is uncertain. Humanity can change that. By curating stories from around the world, our goal is to inspire new ways of thinking, amplify inspiring voices and investigate what the future holds for our home on Earth.

TITLE started as a way of providing a cross section of what’s happening on and to our planet; to better help us all understand the challenges we face today and those that we will face in the future.
